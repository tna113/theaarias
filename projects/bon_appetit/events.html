whipped coffee and japanese soufflé pancakes

The internet is currently going crazy over this new viral drink called “Dalgona Coffee”. This popular coffee drink originated in Korea and now everyone can’t wait to make their own version! It’s a fluffy almost pillow like consistency made entirely of coffee paired with any milk of your choice. The viral drink is nothing shy of a cloudy creamy heaven. And what better to pair it with? Nothing but the most fluffy soufflé pancakes!
